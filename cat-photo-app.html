Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats can make more than 100 different vocal sounds.
  • On average, cats sleep 12–16 hours per day.
  • A group of kittens is called a kindle.

Thinking About Adopting?

If you are interested in giving a cat a loving home, contact your local shelter. Make sure you can provide food, play time, and regular vet visits.

Learn more about responsible adoption on RSPCA’s website.
